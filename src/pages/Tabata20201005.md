**General warm-up**

- Warming up and burpies :)

**Tabata round 1**

- Skater jumps
- Bicycle crunches

**Tabata round 2**

- Scissor kick
- Plank arm & leg raises

**Tabata round 3**

- High knees
- Table top leg cross

**Tabata round 4**

- In & out abs
- Standing triceps

**Tabata round 5**

- Mountain climbers
- Star crunch

**Tabata round 6**

- Squat jumps (front & back)
- Lean forward push-up / Narrow push up

**Tabata round 7**

- 2x Jumping jack -> Plank
- Hips up

**Tabata round 8**

- Side jump with tap
- Knee to elbow (right, left, right)

**Cool down**

- Stretching
