**General warm-up**
- Warming up the body before the workout

- Warrior 3 -> One legged tree

**Combo 3 - cardio**
- Skater -> (GO!) Tuck jump

**Combo 5 - cardio**

- Lunge -> Leg lift -> One legged jump

**Combo 10 - strength**
- Sectional crunch
- Bicycle crunch

**Combo 11 - cardio**
- Plank jump -> Walking plank

**Combo 12 - strength**
- One leg caterpilar -> Side plank

- Stretching
